Demo: 3 Column Layout

1st Content Area

This page demonstrates a 3 column responsive layout, complete with responsive images and jquery slideshow.

Resize the page to see the responsive layout in action.

2nd Content Area

At full width all three columns will be displaed side by side. As the page is resized the third column will collapse under the first and second. At the smallest sceen size all three columns will be stacked on top of one another.
